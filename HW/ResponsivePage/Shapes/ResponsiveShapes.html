Geometric Layouts


I often look for shapes when I'm drafting up a design. Depending on the design I'm working on, geometry can have a huge impact on the way the story is told. Every basic layout starts with a shape, and maybe it even has shapes within shapes like this design to the left here. For my story about color blindness, I wanted to emphasize the jux-to-position of the circles on the left and very squared off text on the right to grab the reader's attention. I used circles throughout the spread to create continuity with shapes. Pictured below is my work.
