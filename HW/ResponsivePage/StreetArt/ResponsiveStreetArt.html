Bold Graffiti


I am constantly using street art styles for inspiration in my own designs. I am drawn to the grungey look it portrays in its rugged quality. Street art is bold, eccentric, dirty, and craves attention for only those who dare get a little dirty with their designs. This picture in particular inspired my work on a magazine cover and table of contents for a class I am in. I used thick letters for the magazine nameplate which pulls from the boldness of the red letters in the grafitti pictured here. I love the contrast of bright red against a dirty wall, which is why I chose to use red and white against a black background. Pictured below is my work.
