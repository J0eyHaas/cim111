Clothing Junky


Clothing Junky, or CJ^3, was created in an Interaction Design class I took in the spring of 2018 at the University of Miami. It's a web as well as mobile application and its main function is to make exploring and organizing clothes much easier for the average consumer. We wanted to create an app that not only customizes clothing suggestions for users based off sizing and survey answers, but also helps users look through their own closet virtually to efficiently find pieces for planning outfits in advance. This app also allows users to explore stores that fit their price range and style so that shopping is easier than ever.
